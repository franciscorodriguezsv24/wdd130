El Salvador

El Salvador, officially the Republic of El Salvador, is the smallest and the most densely populated country in Central America. It is known for its Pacific Ocean beaches, surf spots, and mountainous landscape. The capital city, San Salvador, has numerous museums, galleries, and a vibrant nightlife. El Salvador is rich in culture, history, and natural beauty, making it a unique and fascinating destination.
